Teriyaki Chicken

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken thighs 500g
Soy sauce 100ml
Honey 2 tbsp
Garlic 2 cloves

Step 1: Mix soy sauce, honey, and minced garlic. Marinate chicken for 15 minutes.

Step 2: Cook chicken in a pan until golden and fully cooked.

Step 3: Reduce marinade into a glaze and coat the chicken.

Syrniki with Berries Inside

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Cottage cheese 500g
Eggs 2
Sugar 3 tbsp
Flour 3 tbsp
Berries 1 cup

Step 1: Mix cottage cheese, eggs, sugar, and flour into a smooth dough.

Step 2: Shape into small patties, place berries inside, and seal them well.

Step 3: Fry Syrniki in a pan until golden brown on both sides.
